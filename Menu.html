Appetizers

We sever a verity of Mexican and Cajun appetizers. Starting with our complimentary fresh homemade guacamole.

  • Guacamole: Mashed avocado mixed with lime juice, onions, tomatoes, cilantro, and seasoning, served with tortilla chips.
  • Salsa and Chips: Freshly made tomato-based salsa served with crispy tortilla chips.
  • Nachos: Tortilla chips topped with melted cheese, beans, jalape√±os, sour cream, guacamole, and salsa.
  • Queso Fundido: Melted cheese, often mixed with chorizo or other savory ingredients, served hot with tortillas for dipping.
  • Shrimp Cocktail (Coctel de Camarones): Poached shrimp served in a cocktail sauce with avocado, onions, cilantro, and lime juice.
  • Boudin Balls: Cajun sausage mixed with rice, onions, and spices, formed into balls, breaded, and fried until crispy, served with a spicy dipping sauce.
  • Cajun Crab Dip: Creamy dip made with lump crab meat, cream cheese, Cajun spices, and green onions, served warm with toasted French bread or crackers
  • Cajun Fried Pickles: Crispy dill pickle slices breaded in seasoned cornmeal, fried until golden brown, and served with a spicy dipping sauce.
  • Cajun Crab Cakes: Lump crab meat mixed with Cajun spices, formed into cakes, and pan-fried until golden brown, served with a remoulade sauce.
  • Blackened Catfish Bites: Fresh catfish seasoned with Cajun spices, blackened in a hot skillet, and served with a creamy dipping sauce.
